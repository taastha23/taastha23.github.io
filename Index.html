Popular Destinations

Pokhara

Pokhara is the second-largest city in Nepal and one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country.Located in the foothills of the Himalayas, Pokhara offers stunning views of the Annapurna range and the famous phewa Lake.It is also a gateway to many trekking trails in the Annapurna region.

Manang

Manang(3518 meters) is a beautiful village located at the edge of the cliffs in the north region of the Annapurna Range.The arid landscapes of Mnanag remind us of a barren dessert.The culture and lifestyle of the local Manangi people, on the other hand is vibrant and lively.
Manang is very popular for short trekking options in nepal.The Manang Valley is simply the paradise for trekking, tourist destination.The Manang trek offers you incredible Beauty of natural,rich culture and spectular views of Annapurna ramges.

Bhaktapur

Perched on a hill at altitude of1,401 m,Bhaktapur or Bhadagoan,literally the city of Devotees, is a major tourist destination that takes visitors back in time .Bhaktapur lies 12km to the east of the kathmandu on the Araniko highway that leads to the Chinese border.Covering an area of 6.4sq km,Bhaktapur has managed to retain its authentic ambiance with brick-paved roads, charming red brick houses and a way of life that goes back to mediavial times.The ancient city is also famous for poettry and woodcraving amply displayed on the squares and windows of the local houses and temples.

Chitwan

Chitwan literally means 'heart of the jungle'.The popular Inner Terai Valley gets its name from Chitrasen, the Tharu king, who once ruled here.In the southwest corner of Bagmati Provience, Chitwan lies between foothills of the Himalaya, the Mahabharat and Siwalik ranges. The region called Chitra Ban in earlier refrences used to be dense forest abounding in wild animals and resorted by recluse sages mediating deep in the forests.

In recent years Chitwan National Park tops the list of things to dod in Asia.It is an exciting jungle experience with -jungle safaris, birdwatching,canoe rides and numerous other nature and jungle activities.While the jungles are teeming with wild animals like tigers,leopards and rhinos,along the marshes and rivers and gharial and marsh mugger crocodiles basking in the sun.

Siklis

The Siklis trek is rich in culturally and naturally trail.This trail goes through Gurung villages including untouched blossoming forests of rhododendern in the spring and fothill of Annapurna range.

This trail also enables you to get pleasure from watching the splendid mountain vies of Hiunchuli (6,441m) and Annapurna South (7,270m) and more. The Siklis village is suited above the Mardi Khola, overlooking Annapurna IV to the north and Annapurna II and Lamjung Himal to the east. This trail offers to enjoy bird watching in the forest. Crossing traditional villages and watching superb mountain view makes this Siklis trek more excited.
